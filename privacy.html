1. Who We Are — Data Controller

This Privacy Policy ("Notice") describes how Cielo Cell AB, org. no. 559579-6912, DUNS 373683881, Målarevägen 24, 227 30 Lund, Sweden ("CieloCell," "we," "us," "our") collects, uses, shares, and protects your personal data when you use our mobile application, website, and eSIM connectivity services (the "Service").

Cielo Cell AB is the data controller for all personal data processed under this Notice. Where we engage third parties to process data on our behalf, they do so under binding data processing agreements that require them to protect your information to at least the same standard we do.

For questions about this Notice, contact our privacy team at privacy@cielocell.com.

2. What Personal Data We Collect

2.1 Data You Provide Directly

  • Account registration: Name, email address, hashed password, and authentication tokens (Google/Apple ID).
  • Payment information: Billing name, card type, last four digits, expiry date, and billing address. Full card numbers are processed and stored solely by our PCI DSS-certified payment processor. CieloCell never stores raw card data.
  • Communications: Messages and attachments you send via support channels, email, or in-app chat.
  • Survey responses: Answers and feedback submitted through the Platform's survey or offer features.

2.2 Data We Collect Automatically

  • Device identifiers: Device model, OS version, unique device ID, advertising ID (IDFA on iOS / GAID on Android), and eSIM EID.
  • Network data: IP address, mobile network code, roaming status, and connection type.
  • App usage: Screen views, feature interactions, session duration, ad impressions, ad completions, Points earned and redeemed, and data plan activations.
  • Location data: Country-level location derived from IP address or SIM registration, used solely to determine eligible data plans. We do not collect precise GPS coordinates unless you expressly grant location permission for coverage-checking features.
  • Crash and diagnostic data: Error logs, stack traces, and performance metrics to maintain service quality.

2.3 Data from Third Parties

  • Authentication providers: If you sign in via Google or Apple, we receive your name, email, and authentication token from that provider.
  • Advertising partners: Ad viewability signals, completion events, and fraud-prevention signals from ad network SDKs embedded in the app.
  • MVNO network partners: Data consumption records and eSIM activation status necessary to provision your service.

4. The Ad-Subsidized Model — Advertising Data

CieloCell's free-data model is powered by advertising revenue. For advertisers to pay us for ad delivery, we share certain device-level signals with our ad network partners. This is fundamental to how the Service works and we are committed to being transparent about it.

When you watch an ad in the CieloCell app:

  • Your device's advertising identifier (IDFA / GAID), country, device type, and ad completion signal are shared with the ad network serving that ad.
  • Depending on your device settings, ad networks may use your advertising ID to show you relevant advertising across other apps and services (interest-based advertising).
  • You can limit ad tracking or reset your advertising ID at any time:
    • iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → toggle off "Allow Apps to Request to Track"
    • Android: Settings → Privacy → Ads → "Delete advertising ID" or "Opt out of Ads Personalization"
  • Opting out of ad tracking means ads will still be shown but will be less relevant. Your ability to earn Points is not affected by this choice.

CieloCell does not share your name, email address, or payment information with advertisers.

5. Who We Share Your Data With

5.1 Service Providers (Processors)

We engage trusted third-party vendors who process data only on our instructions under binding data processing agreements:

  • Cloud infrastructure: Hosting, databases, and CDN (e.g. AWS, Google Cloud Platform).
  • Payment processors: PCI DSS Level 1 certified providers (e.g. Stripe). No raw card data is accessed by CieloCell.
  • Analytics: App performance and crash analytics (e.g. Firebase Crashlytics, Amplitude) — data is pseudonymised where possible.
  • Customer support: Helpdesk and ticketing software.
  • Email delivery: Transactional and marketing email providers.

5.2 MVNO Network Partners

To provision your eSIM and deliver mobile data, we share your device EID and plan details with our licensed MVNO partner in your country. These partners process data under their own privacy policies and applicable telecommunications law. We contractually require MVNO partners to maintain appropriate data protection standards.

5.3 Advertising Partners

As described in Section 4, we share device-level advertising signals with ad network partners. We do not share personally identifiable information (name, email, payment data) with advertisers.

5.4 Legal Requirements

We may disclose personal data if required by law, court order, or governmental authority, or if we believe disclosure is necessary to: comply with a legal obligation; protect the rights or safety of CieloCell, our users, or the public; or detect, prevent, or address fraud or security issues. We disclose only the minimum data required and will notify you where legally permitted.

5.5 Business Transfers

In a merger, acquisition, asset sale, or restructuring of Cielo Cell AB, your data may transfer to the successor entity. We will provide prior notice where practicable and ensure the successor is bound to equivalent data protection standards.

5.6 With Your Consent

We may share data with third parties for purposes not described in this Notice where we have your explicit, informed, freely given consent.

6. International Data Transfers

Cielo Cell AB is headquartered in Sweden (EU). Some of our service providers and MVNO partners operate in countries outside the EU/EEA. Where personal data is transferred to such countries, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, including:

  • EU Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) as approved by the European Commission (Decision 2021/914);
  • UK International Data Transfer Agreements (IDTAs) where applicable;
  • Transfers to countries with an EU adequacy decision (e.g. Japan, Canada, New Zealand).

You may request a copy of the applicable safeguards by contacting privacy@cielocell.com.

9. California Residents — CCPA / CPRA

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) as amended by the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA) grants you additional rights.

Categories of Personal Information Collected (preceding 12 months)

  • Identifiers (name, email, device ID, IP address, advertising ID);
  • Commercial information (purchase history, data plan activations, Points balance);
  • Internet / electronic activity (app usage, ad interaction, session data);
  • Geolocation data (country-level only);
  • Inferences drawn from the above to create a profile for ad relevance.

Sale or Sharing of Personal Information

CieloCell does not sell personal information for money. However, sharing device advertising identifiers with ad network partners may constitute "sharing" for cross-context behavioural advertising under CPRA. You have the right to opt out of this sharing.

To opt out: reset or disable your advertising ID on your device (iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking; Android: Settings → Privacy → Ads), or email privacy@cielocell.com with the subject "Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information."

Your California Rights

  • Right to Know: Categories and specific pieces of personal information collected, sources, business purposes, and third-party disclosures.
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of personal information, subject to exceptions.
  • Right to Correct: Request correction of inaccurate personal information.
  • Right to Opt Out: Opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information for cross-context behavioural advertising.
  • Right to Limit Use of Sensitive Personal Information: Limit our use to service provision where applicable.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not deny, charge differently for, or provide a different level of service for exercising CCPA rights.

To submit a verifiable consumer request, email privacy@cielocell.com from your registered address. We will respond within 45 days, extendable by 45 more days with notice.

11. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies (pixels, local storage) to: operate essential site functions; remember preferences; analyze traffic; and serve advertising. Types used:

  • Strictly necessary: Required for site functionality. Cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics: Help us understand how visitors interact with the site (e.g. Google Analytics). Opt out via your browser settings or the Google Analytics opt-out add-on.

You can manage cookies through your browser's privacy settings. Disabling certain cookies may affect website functionality. Where required by law (e.g. ePrivacy Directive), we obtain consent before placing non-essential cookies.
